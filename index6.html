Ingredients

1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour

¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder

2 teaspoons baking powder

¼ teaspoon baking soda

⅛ teaspoon salt

1 ½ cups white sugar

3 tablespoons butter, softened

2 large eggs

¾ teaspoon vanilla extract

1 cup milk

cupcakes

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a muffin pan with paper or foil liners.

Step 2

Sift together flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

Step 3

Cream together sugar and butter in a large bowl until light and fluffy.

Step 4

Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla.
Add flour mixture in two batches,alternating with milk; beat well.

Step 5

Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 3/4 full.

Step 6

Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the centers comes out clean,
15 to 17 minutes.Remove from the oven and let cool before serving or frosting
