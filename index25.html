Ingredients

1 ½ pounds salmon fillets

lemon pepper to taste.

garlic powder to taste.

salt to taste.

⅓ cup soy sauce

⅓ cup brown sugar

⅓ cup water

¼ cup vegetable oil

Salmon

Step 1

Season salmon fillets with lemon pepper, garlic powder, and salt

Step 2

Stir soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil together in a small bowl until sugar
is dissolved. Place fish in a large resealable plastic bag; add soy sauce mixture,seal,
and turn to coat. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

Step 3

Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.

Step 4

Place salmon on the preheated grill, and discard marinade.
Cook salmon until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 6 to 8 minutes per side.
