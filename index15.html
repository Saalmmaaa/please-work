Ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour

2 tablespoons white sugar

2 teaspoons baking powder

1 teaspoon salt, or to taste

1 cup milk

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

1 egg, beaten

Pan cakes

Step 1

Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, oil, and egg. Mix until smooth.

Step 2

Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour
or scoop batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake;
cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 1 to 2 minutes.
Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter

Step 3

Serve hot and enjoy!
