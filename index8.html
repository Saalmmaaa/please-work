Ingredients

1 cup butter flavored shortening

¾ cup white sugar

¾ cup brown sugar

2 large eggs

2 teaspoons vanilla extract

2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking soda

1 teaspoon salt

¾ 2 cups milk chocolate chips

cookies

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 2 cookie sheets.

Step 2

Cream together butter-flavored shortening, brown sugar, and white sugar in a large bowl until
light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition; stir in vanilla.

Step 3

Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl; gradually stir into creamed mixture.

Step 4

Fold in chocolate chips until well combined.

Step 5

Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto the prepared cookie sheets.

Step 6

Bake in the preheated oven until light brown, 8 to 10 minutes. Allow cookies to cool
on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely
