Ingredients

1 cup warm water
(110 degrees F/45 degrees C).

1 (.25 ounce) package active
dry yeast.

1 teaspoon white sugar.

2 Â½ cups bread flour.

2 tablespoons olive oil.

1 teaspoon salt.

Pizza

Step 1

Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C), and lightly grease a pizza pan.

Step 2

Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.

Step 3

Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth. You can do this by hand or use
a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook to make it easier.

Step 4

Let rest for 5 minutes.

Step 5

Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.

Step 6

Transfer to the prepared pizza pan.

Step 7

Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice.

Step 8

Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and
let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
