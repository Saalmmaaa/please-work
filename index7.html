Ingredients

cup white sugar

½ cup butter

2 large eggs

2 teaspoons vanilla extract

1 ½ cups all-purpose flour

1 ¾ teaspoons baking powder

¾ cup milk

1 tablespoon lemon zest

1 tablespoon lemon juice

Lemon Cake

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch square baking pan

Step 2

Beat sugar and butter together in a mixing bowl using an electric mixer until light and fluffy.
Beat in eggs and vanilla extract until well combined.

Step 3

Sift flour and baking powder together in a separate bowl;
add to creamed mixture until incorporated

Step 4

Pour in milk, lemon zest, and lemon juice; mix until smooth.

Step 5

Spoon batter into the prepared pan.

Step 6

Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean,
about 35 minutes
